She is also a former 2 year member of the New York University Women’s Volleyball Team and a member of various campus-based clubs and communities. Working towards a Bachelor of Computer Science and a secondary speciality in Game Design, she is eager to expand her knowledge in both fields and ultimately pursue a career that allows her to combine both technological and creative features.
